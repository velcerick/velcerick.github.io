Felibwyn - Grove King

Lesser God
Titles: Grove King, Thistle
Portfolio: Fields, Groves, Woodland Plains
Favored Weapon: Scythe
Symbol: A thistle flower

Felibwyn, or Thistle as he is known by most mortals, claims responsiblity for the creation of the fruiting trees of Elindra. His mother is Nydilal and his father Seram and his relationship with his mother ensures that when he needs rain for his pursuits he has but to ask. He is most often worshipped by those who tend orchards and groves.

Personality: Felibwyn, it is said, works with the speed of a growing tree. That is to say, he works slowly and deliberately, often answering prayers or completing tasks only long after their need has passed. When interacting directly with mortals he tends to lose focus, to ponder, and to frequently pepper his speech with long pauses.

Description: Felibwyn is usually depicted as an elderly man with a long white beard and large brimmed straw hat, most often stitting on the branch of a tree. He generally has a smile on his face, but prefers not to come down from his perch.
